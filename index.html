Usage Guide

Learn how to make the most out of our weather website

Introduction

Welcome to our Weather Website! This platform provides real-time weather information, including temperature, "feels like" temperature, UV index, humidity, rain intensity, weather conditions, wind speed, visibility, and atmospheric pressure for any location.

Getting Started

  1. Accessing the Website: Open your web browser and enter the website URL to access the platform.
  2. Homepage Overview:
    • Search Bar: Located at the top of the page, this allows you to enter the name of the city or location you want to check the weather for.

Understanding the Weather Data

  • Temperature: Shows the current temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • Feels Like: Indicates what the temperature feels like due to factors like humidity and wind speed.
  • UV Index: Provides the current UV index to help you understand the level of sun exposure risk.
  • Humidity: Displays the percentage of humidity in the air.
  • Rain Intensity: Shows the current intensity of rainfall in millimeters per hour.
  • Weather Condition: Describes the current weather (e.g., sunny, cloudy, rainy).
  • Wind Speed: Indicates the speed of the wind in kilometers per hour.
  • Visibility: Shows the distance you can clearly see, measured in kilometers.
  • Pressure: Displays the atmospheric pressure in millibars (hPa).
